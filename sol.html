Sol

  • Made in: 2021
  • Time spent: 8 weeks
  • Engine: Godot

Sol is an adventure/fantasy RPG that was created by me and two team members in 2021 for our Passion Project at CMD. This was the first time making a videogame for everyone in the team. We had about 8 weeks to come up with a concept, setting and gameplay features and to actually make the game. In the end we managed to create one medium sized level with multiple areas, NPC's to talk to, different enemy types to fight and of course a classic boss fight at the end.

While other team members focused on conceptualization and asset creation my main focus during this project was on the logic behind the game. I had to quickly learn to work with the Godot game engine and also learn to write in GDScript. From creating the world, to adding working animations and sound effects to eventually creating enemy AI and projectiles.

All in all this was an enormously fun undertaking and one that I definitely learnt a lot from. It's simply very rewarding seeing your ideas slowly come to live and become playable and each additional part that gets added is met with excitement from the whole team!
