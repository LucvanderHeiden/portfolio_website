Animated lyrics video

  • Made in: 2022
  • Time spent: 2 weeks
  • Program: Adobe After Effects

I made a animated lyric video for a graphic design class in 2022. The video is made in portrait layout so that it could be used in mobile music apps like Spotify or Apple Music. I chose the song "Show Me" by San Holo. The song is quite hopeful and a bit dreamy, that's why I decided to go with bright colors and a dreamy landscape in the form of clouds in the sky.

The sky and the clouds in the background of the video are completely computer generated using Adobe After Effects. After that, the animated lyrics were made using a combination of Adobe After Effects and Premiere Pro. This process involved a lot of work because I had to manually trace all the lyrics, letter for letter.

I've always had an interest in motion-graphics and video-editing, that started with me editing gaming videos for fun. I'd never really done much with After Effects before however, so it was fun to dive into a new program and learn something new. I also liked that I managed to make this animated landscape from scratch and it worked really well for the video.
