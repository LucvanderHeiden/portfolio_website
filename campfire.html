3D Animated campfire

  • Made in: 2022
  • Time spent: 2 weeks
  • Program: Blender

In 2022 I enrolled in an elective course on 3D modelling and animation. Here I learnt the basics of creating and working with 3D models within Blender. As I've always been interested in game development it was really interesting to me to learn about this, as obviously 3D work is quite essential to many games.

After making many small assignments to practice we ended the course with two weeks time to model and animate a scene of our own choosing. I chose to make a campfire in the woods with stars in the background, inspired by my favorite videogame Outer Wilds. I had learnt a lot over the course of this semester and I had to apply all this new knowledge during the making of this final scene.

I'm very happy I chose to do this elective course because I now have a very good foundational understanding of 3D modelling and animating. Next to that it was also simply a really fun course and it has allowed me to now see 3D as an option to go in during other design projects.
