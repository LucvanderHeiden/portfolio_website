Official Formula 1 website recreation

  • Made in: 2021
  • Time spent: 4 weeks
  • Language: HTML/CSS/JS

For one of my front-end classes I was tasked with recreating an existing website as closely as possible. I chose to use the official Formula 1 website because I thought it would be a good challenge from which I could learn a lot.

I tried to recreate the website as closely as possible. The website has many interesting small design choices and also includes a lot of CSS animations and transitions to make the website feel more interactive. These animations (mostly hover animations) I also incorporated in my own recreation of the website. The recreation is also relatively responsive, with it nicely adapting to a mobile screen. Here it changes the navigation bar into a hamburger menu and all of the content is either moved or made smaller to fit into the smaller screen of mobile phones.

In the end, working on this project thaught me something new. It thaught me how to look at existing websites and components as a developer, analysing how certains elements are most likely to have been coded and how to recreate something similar for myself. Below you can see the end result in the form of a comparison between the official version and my recreation of the website. (Keep in mind that my recreation of the website was made in 2021 while the images of the official website were made in 2023 and some of the page design might have slightly been alterred).
