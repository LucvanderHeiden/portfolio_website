Cornfield Massacre

  • Made in: 2023
  • Time spent: 4 weeks
  • Engine: Unity

NOTE: Use the blue fullscreen icon to go into proper fullscreen.

Cornfield Massacre is a horror game that I made together with three others during the Applied Game Design minor I took at the AUAS in the beginning of 2023. The game was made in rather short 4 weeks. The final product is a short game that tells a story from beginning to end by using a combination of gameplay and cutscenes.

Our team of four members was divided into one asset designer, one game designer and two game developers, one of which was me. We faced a challenge of making a top-down game scary, but we tried to make this happen by using minimal lighting, good sound design and above all and unpredictable main enemy. The development of this enemy was where most of my focus was during this project. I worked hard on creating pathfinding for the enemy so it could walk around in our levels. Since we didn't have a lot of time or any real resources I came up with a creative solution for enemy AI so that it was unpredictable, surprising and above all, scary.

This was a very interesting project to work on because it was unlike any other game I had worked on before. This time there was really a lot of extra focus on the story, setting and atmosphere. It was also the first time that I worked on more interesting and in-depth enemy behavior. In the end I learnt a lot about the importance of good and fun enemy behavior that is challenging while still being fair to the player.
