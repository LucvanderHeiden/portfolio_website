The Descent

  • Made in: 2023
  • Time spent: 5 weeks
  • Engine: Unity

NOTE: Use the blue fullscreen icon to go into proper fullscreen.

The Descent is a game I made on my own and it's also the first game I ever made in the Unity game engine. We were given 5 weeks time to learn about the engine, get familiar with C# and comlete our game. I knew with the limited time and experience making everything from scratch would be too much to ask so I used an animated player and pixel-art backgrounds from the Unity Store. However, all the other pixel-art including enemies, items, platforms, checkpoints, menu screens and other UI elements were all made by me. This was also my first time creating pixel-art and it was something I really enjoyed doing.

During the development of this game I felt inspired by one of my favorite games "Hollow Knight". That's why I decided to make a small metroidvania that could easily be completed in one sitting. I focused on making the movement around the world fun with each additional upgrade giving the player more freedom in mobility. The setting was inspired by Lovecraftian themes and the story of the game is quite simple: Your character accidentally falls into a large cave system. There is seemingly no way to get out again. Only being going deeper into the caves will you be able to unlock new abilities that will eventually allow you to escape the abyss.

The end result was something I was quite proud of especially given the short time we had to complete it. The combat mechanics of the game are still lacking a bit and I wish I would've had more time to fine tune these to make the combat equally as fun as the movement. Still the project was quite the success and was graded by my teachers as a perfect 10.
